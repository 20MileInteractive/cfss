Intro/Fundamentals

This program meets three times a week for two weeks. It begins on the first and third Monday of every month. During these initial sessions you will be exposed to a variety of exercise movements at light to moderate intensity levels while you become familiar with our methods. We are training movements, not muscles. We are primarily looking for competency with these specific movement patterns.
