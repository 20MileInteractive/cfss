David Mesheau

David Mesheau has over twenty years of fitness coaching experience on the South Shore. It includes athletes of all ages and backgrounds. His passion for fitness began in his youth with a goal to become a competitive bodybuilder. In 1994 he decided to focus on personal training as his profession. After many years as a successful personal trainer and fitness coach, he added CrossFit to his training philosophy and method.

Dave continues to modify his own beliefs regarding fitness attainment in a never ending quest to improve his abilities and the abilities of those he trains.

  • USAW Level 1 Sports Performance Coach
  • CrossFit Level 1(CF-L1) Trainer
  • CPR Certified

Bonnie Walsh

I was first introduced to CrossFit in 2007 after having suffered a repetitive use injury from running. Having graduated from Bridgewater State University with a Bachelor’s degree in Criminal Justice, I was hired on a local Department and was training for the police academy. Because I was unable to run anymore, I was in search of a new way to train. Always having been a competitive athlete, I was constantly seeking physical activities that were new and challenging.

CrossFit turned out to be everything I was looking for, and then some. I was initially drawn to the CrossFit program due to its easy transference to “real world” situations. After regular participation in CrossFit classes, I noticed incredible gains in my strength and stamina, as well as my confidence. It propelled my baseline fitness level to new heights I didn’t know were possible for me, all the while giving me the friendly competition and teammate camaraderie that I had always loved from sports. Upon graduating the 6 month long police academy in 2009 I had broken multiple PT records (including a running record!) all of which I credit entirely to my CrossFit training.

Because I had personally benefitted so much from the CrossFit method, I later became a certified CrossFit level 1 trainer, as well as a certified USAW Sports Performance Coach. Being able to share my love for CrossFit with others is such a fantastic feeling because I know how well the program works. I am so grateful for CrossFit and I look forward to sharing it with you.

  • USAW Level 1 Sports Performance Coach
  • CrossFit Level 1(CF-L1) Trainer
  • CPR Certified
