About CrossFit South Shore

Powered by CrossFit training methods, CrossFit South Shore offers a program of simple, effective strength and conditioning workouts to deliver time tested measurable health and fitness improvements for athletes and people of all ages and fitness levels.

Not Your Average Gym

CrossFit South Shore is a one of a kind CrossFit Box on the South Shore (Located Behind Stop&Shop Rt 53). We have Mens and Womens locker rooms with showers and handicap access. The exercise area is climate controlled and fully equipped with functional fitness equipment.
