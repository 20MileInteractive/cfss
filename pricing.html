Intro To CrossFit

Intro program runs for 6 sessions total. (3 sessions per week for 2 weeks.) It begins on the first and third Monday of every month and includes access to open gym.

During these initial sessions you will be exposed to a variety of exercise movements at light to moderate intensity levels while you become familiar with our methods. We are training movements, not muscles. We are primarily looking for competency with these specific movement patterns.

All new members are required to complete this intro program.
