Thursday, July 11, 2013

  • Front squat 3-3-3-1-1-1
  • Rest 2-5 Min.
  • For Time:
    • 100 Burpee squat (1 Burpee + 1 Air squat)
      With a continuous running clock, every 5 min stop and run 400m. Continue with Burpee squat, stopping and running 400m at every 5 min interval until all 100 Burpee squats are completed. Record time and number of 400m runs.
